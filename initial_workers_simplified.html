Welcome

Thank you for taking part in this study. We are investigating how people perform on task efficiency. Your participation helps us understand decision-making in real-world scenarios.

How this works:

  • You will complete a brief performance task that requires focus and attention.
  • Some participants will be selected for payment based on their task performance.
  • If selected, another participant will decide how to distribute $8 between you and another worker.
  • We will then contact the selected participants via email to inform them of their earnings.

The task will take approximately 2–3 minutes to complete.

Participant Information & Consent
This study is conducted by Isabella Valencia at the Paris School of Economics, under the supervision of Professor Andrea FM Martinangeli. Your participation is entirely voluntary and you may withdraw at any time by closing this browser window. Data is collected anonymously under GDPR regulations and will be used for academic purposes only. Your email will only be used to contact you if you are selected for payment. Part 1: The Task

You will be presented with a grid of three-digit numbers.
At the top of the grid, you will see a target code (e.g., "123").
You must find and check off all instances of that code in the grid.

Accuracy matters:
Correct markings score 1 point each.
Incorrect markings lose 1 point each.

You will complete 5 rounds, each lasting 20 seconds.

Press >> to get a simplified example and a practice round before starting the real task.
